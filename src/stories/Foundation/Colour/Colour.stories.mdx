# Colour

Colour themes are designed to be harmonious, and accessible UI elements. Maintaining consistent digital interface throughout starts with the right use of colours. The following are the foundations used to achieve a consistent user interface.

## Primary colours

A primary color is the color displayed most frequently across your app's screens and components.

## Neutral colours

A secondary color provides more ways to accent and distinguish your product. Having a secondary color is optional, and should be applied sparingly to accent select parts of your UI.
